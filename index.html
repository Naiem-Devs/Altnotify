WHAT IS ALTNOTIFY

altnotify is a smartphone app currently for android only, yet an ios app in the works. altnotify is a tool to help anyone involved or interested in cryptocurrencies. altnotify serves has one main objective as an app, to notify the user's device of any new altcoin which has been released, the moment it gets released on any exchange.

TIRED OF WAITING FOR ANNOUNCEMENTS

AltNotify scans for new altcoins every minute using a complex API connecting to various exchanges to alert you the moment a new coin is released to an exchange, this cuts out the need for checking telegrams, reading exchanges news and Twitter pages and allowed you the possibility to be in the know without wasting your day scouring for information. altnotify is not intended to sell or promote any crypto and has no security risks nor connects to any of your assets, altnotify does exactly what it says in the name, notify you when it matters saving you time and giving you the edge. If you are a trader this would be the perfect tool to ensure you are in first with new coins released if you are just an altcoin follower it would give you the ability to get stuck into your research on said coin instantly.

This app is a tool, made by traders to help save you time. And best of all, it is free!
